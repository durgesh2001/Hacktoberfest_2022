Welcome to our Website

The main aim of this website is to help the aspiring high school and undergraduate students to find the most suitable universities for their study.

About Us

During their lifetime, most students who complete a decent level of education has to go through the hurdle of finding the right college which focus on their morals and spirations.



Due to lack of knowledge and public opinion a great number of them end up regretting later in life by enrolling in something which wasn’t meant for them. With the progress of internet through outthe country, in recent years.



During our times, we had to endure such problems and the only solution we could find was speaking directly with the current students of a particular university we’re hoping to get in. To cater this problem, we’re working on this project.



The primary objective is to create a website which allows every user to create their own personalized profile so that each list is unique and individuality of the collection of colleges based on their interest can be achieved.
